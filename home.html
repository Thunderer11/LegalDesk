Adv. Samridhi Sharma

Adv. Samridhi Sharma is a dedicated legal professional with a strong interest in legal research, legal writing, and litigation-related work. With an analytical and research-oriented approach towards law, she focuses on providing clear legal insights and promoting awareness about important legal rights and contemporary legal issues.

She holds a B.Com LL.B. (Hons.) degree from Chandigarh University and has developed significant experience in legal drafting, legal research, and case law analysis. Her work involves drafting legal documents such as legal notices, applications, affidavits, agreements, and other legal pleadings while conducting in-depth legal research using leading legal databases.

Apart from legal practice, Adv. Samridhi Sharma actively contributes to legal scholarship and academic research. She is the author of the book "Family Law: An Overview to Hindu Law." Her research papers have been published in reputed journals including IJCRT, IJRAR, IJIRT, and Lex Scholasticus, and she has also authored multiple book chapters on various contemporary legal subjects.

She has presented research papers at national conferences organized by leading law institutions across India, contributing to discussions on emerging legal challenges and developments in the legal field.

Her professional interests primarily include litigation, constitutional law, legal research, and emerging areas of law. She remains committed to ethical legal practice, professional integrity, and the continuous pursuit of legal knowledge.
